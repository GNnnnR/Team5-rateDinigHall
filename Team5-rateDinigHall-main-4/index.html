About TritonEats

TritonEats is a website designed to help Tritons explore campus dining halls. UCSD has many dining halls across campus, but it can be hard to know which location has the best meals, restaurants, or menu items.

This website organizes UCSD dining halls by location, shows the restaurants inside each dining hall, and provides descriptions and images of each restaurant. Tritons can also rate restaurants based on taste, value, and overall experience.

By collecting student ratings, TritonEats helps Tritons make better dining choices and gives a clearer picture of which foods are most popular across the campus.

UCSD Dining Halls

64 Degrees

Location: 64 Degrees - Revelle College

Located in Revelle College, 64 Degrees is named after the average temperature of the Pacific Ocean at La Jolla. It offers a wide range of food stations including a grill, Asian wok, taqueria, sushi bar, pasta station, and garden salad bar. With its vibrant atmosphere and diverse menu, 64 Degrees is a favorite dining destination for students living in and around Revelle College.

View Today's Menu

Restaurants inside 64 Degrees

64 Triton Grill

64 Triton Grill is the classic comfort food station at 64 Degrees, serving made-to-order burgers, crispy fries, chicken sandwiches, and rotating daily specials. It is one of the most popular spots in the dining hall, known for its hearty portions and satisfying flavors. Students also love stopping by for the soft-serve ice cream, which makes a great end to any meal.

Wok

Wok brings bold Asian flavors to 64 Degrees with a rotating weekly menu that keeps things fresh and exciting. Expect stir-fries, fried rice, noodle dishes, and flavorful sauces inspired by Chinese, Thai, and other Asian cuisines. The menu changes regularly so there is always something new to try throughout the quarter.

Taqueria

Taqueria is 64 Degrees' Mexican-inspired station, offering a flavorful lineup of tacos, burritos, rice bowls, and sides. Students can choose from a variety of proteins, toppings, and salsas to customize their meal. The station also features rotating seasonal desserts, making it a well-rounded stop for lunch or dinner.

Garden Bar

Garden Bar is the health-focused station at 64 Degrees, perfect for students looking for a lighter, fresher meal. It offers build-your-own salads with a wide variety of greens, toppings, and dressings, as well as hearty soups and made-to-order sandwiches. The station is a popular choice for students who want a balanced and nutritious option without sacrificing flavor.

Umi

Umi is 64 Degrees' dedicated sushi station, serving freshly made sushi rolls, nigiri, and rice bowls. It is one of the most sought-after spots in the dining hall, known for its quality ingredients and creative flavor combinations. The station also offers special desserts, making it a complete dining experience for sushi lovers on campus.

Al Dente

Al Dente is 64 Degrees' Italian pasta station, serving a rotating weekly selection of pasta dishes including spaghetti, penne, and other classic styles with a variety of sauces and toppings. Each dish is prepared fresh and seasoned generously, offering a warm and filling meal. The changing menu ensures there is always a new combination to enjoy throughout the week.

Overall 64 Degrees Rating

Café Ventanas

Location: Cafe Ventanas - ERC College

Located in ERC College, Café Ventanas is one of UCSD's most culturally diverse dining halls, featuring globally inspired food stations that rotate through Caribbean, Indian, African, and other world cuisines. Opened in 2024 after a major renovation, it also offers a coffeehouse and a variety of grab-and-go options. Its vibrant atmosphere and unique menu make it a standout dining destination on campus.

View Today's Menu

Restaurants inside Café Ventanas

HaPi

HaPi is Café Ventanas' signature handpie station, serving freshly baked savory pastries with a rotating selection of fillings. Each handpie is made with flaky crust and hearty ingredients, making it a satisfying and portable meal option that is popular for students on the go between classes.

Soul

Soul is Café Ventanas' Southern comfort food station, serving classic American soul food including fried chicken, mac and cheese, collard greens, and cornbread. The generous portions and rich, home-style flavors make it one of the most comforting and popular stops in the dining hall.

Journey

Journey is Café Ventanas' globally inspired African and Middle Eastern station, featuring bold and aromatic flavors. The menu includes beef kofta, chicken harissa, flatbread, and other rotating specialties drawn from across the African continent and beyond. It is a great option for students looking to explore diverse and richly spiced cuisines.

Tandoor

Tandoor is Café Ventanas' Indian cuisine station, named after the traditional clay oven used to cook its signature dishes. The menu features tandoor-roasted meats, freshly baked naan, and fragrant curries with a variety of spice levels. It is a favorite among students who enjoy authentic, deeply flavorful South Asian cooking.

Vibe

Vibe is Café Ventanas' Caribbean-inspired station, bringing the bold and sunny flavors of the islands to campus. The menu includes jerk chicken, rice and peas, sweet plantains, and other rotating Caribbean staples. The vibrant seasoning and tropical ingredients make Vibe one of the most distinctive and lively stations in the dining hall.

Kaldi

Kaldi is Café Ventanas' coffeehouse station, named after the legendary Ethiopian goat herder credited with discovering coffee. It serves espresso drinks, drip coffee, teas, and rotating seasonal beverages. It is the perfect spot to grab a morning coffee or an afternoon pick-me-up before or after classes.

Overall Ventanas Rating

OceanView

Location: OceanView - Marshall College

Located in Marshall College, Oceanview Terrace is known for its scenic canyon views and diverse food offerings. The dining hall features a specialty stone-fired pizza oven, a Mediterranean and Kosher-certified kitchen, Italian dishes, and a grab-and-go café with pastries and espresso drinks. It is a popular gathering spot for students seeking quality meals with a relaxed, open atmosphere.

View Today's Menu

Restaurants inside OceanView

Counter Culture

Counter Culture is OceanView's café and dessert station, serving house-baked pastries, gelato, and acai bowls. It also offers espresso drinks, a variety of fruit smoothies, and toasted bagels with unique spreads. It is a popular stop for students looking for a sweet treat or a quick caffeinated pick-me-up between classes.

Spice (Kosher Kitchen)

Spice is OceanView's Kosher-certified Mediterranean station, offering garlic fries bowls, Greek salads, and mazza bowls alongside other Middle Eastern and North African inspired dishes. It is one of the few Kosher dining options on campus, making it an important and well-loved destination for students with specific dietary needs.

Scholar's Pizza

Scholar's Pizza is OceanView's stone-fired pizza station, serving freshly made pizzas with a rotating selection of toppings and sauces. Students can also build and customize their own pizza, making it a fun and flexible option for any taste. The crispy, fire-baked crust is a big draw for pizza lovers on campus.

Scholar's Italian

Scholar's Italian is OceanView's pasta station, serving a rotating selection of classic Italian dishes including pastas and spaghetti with a variety of sauces and toppings. Students can also build their own salads at this station, making it a great all-in-one option for a hearty and customizable meal.

Overall OceanView Rating

Foodworx

Location: Foodworx - Pepper Canyon

Located in the Pepper Canyon area near the center of campus, Foodworx serves as a convenient dining hub for students from multiple colleges. It offers a rotating selection of globally inspired cuisines in a fast-casual format, making it an ideal stop between classes or during busy school days. Its central location makes it one of the most accessible dining spots on campus.

View Today's Menu

Restaurants inside Foodworx

Foodworx Impinger

The Impinger is Foodworx's signature high-heat conveyor oven station, turning out made-to-order flatbreads, pizzas, and toasted sandwiches in minutes. The fast-fire process gives each item a satisfying crispy finish while keeping the inside warm and fresh. It's one of the quickest hot-meal options at Foodworx, perfect for students with back-to-back classes.

Foodworx Deli & Salad

The Deli & Salad station at Foodworx offers build-your-own sandwiches, wraps, and fresh salads with a wide selection of proteins, cheeses, vegetables, and dressings. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty deli sandwich or a light customized salad, this station has flexible options to suit any appetite. It's a go-to for students looking for a fresher, lighter meal.

Overall Foodworx Rating

Seventh College Dining

Location: Seventh Market — Seventh College

Located in Seventh College, Seventh College Dining is one of UCSD's newest dining facilities, opened alongside the college in 2021. It features a modern dining experience with a coffeehouse, a deli serving hot sandwiches and soups, and a bistro offering globally inspired small plates and sushi. The dining hall embodies the diverse and forward-thinking spirit of Seventh College.

View Today's Menu

Restaurants inside Seventh College Dining

Seventh Coffeehouse

Seventh Coffeehouse is the café station at Seventh College Dining, serving a variety of lattes, teas, shakers, and seasonal drinks. It also offers bagels, pastries, and other light snacks, making it a great spot for a quick breakfast or an afternoon coffee break between classes.

Northside Deli

Northside Deli is Seventh College Dining's quick-service deli station, serving hot sandwiches and soups made to order. It is an ideal stop for students looking for a warm, satisfying meal they can take on the go. The hearty sandwiches and rotating soups make it a reliable and popular option throughout the day.

Bistro

Bistro is Seventh College Dining's globally inspired small plates station, offering a rotating menu of bold and creative dishes. Favorites include Korean BBQ wings, dumplings in chili oil, udon, and desserts like Thai mango sticky rice. The diverse and exciting menu makes Bistro one of the most popular and talked-about spots at Seventh College Dining.

Bistro Sushi

Bistro Sushi is Seventh College Dining's dedicated sushi station, offering a rotating selection of freshly made sushi rolls and other Japanese bites. It is one of the most sought-after stations in the dining hall, so expect lines during peak meal times. The quality and variety of the rolls make the wait well worth it.

Overall Seventh College Dining Rating

Sixth College Dining

Location: Sixth College Dining — Sixth College

Located in Sixth College, Sixth College Dining is one of the most modern dining areas at UC San Diego. It offers a variety of restaurants with different styles of food, including Mexican-inspired fare, Hawaiian bowls, comfort food, and a convenience market. With its diverse options and lively atmosphere, it is a popular place for students to eat, study, and hang out with friends.

View Today's Menu

Restaurants inside Sixth College

Sixth Market

Sixth Market is a convenient grocery-style dining spot for students who need quick meals, snacks, and drinks throughout the day. The market carries instant noodles, sandwiches, salads, yogurt, frozen meals, desserts, and many beverage options.

Many students stop by Sixth Market late at night because it is fast and easy to grab food between studying or classes. It is also a great place to buy small essentials and quick breakfast items.

Sixth Coffeehouse

Sixth Coffeehouse is the go-to spot for coffee lovers at Sixth College, serving espresso drinks, lattes, teas, and seasonal beverages. It also offers a selection of pastries and light snacks, making it a perfect stop before morning classes or for a midday study break.

Noodle

Noodle is Sixth College's dedicated noodle station, serving a rotating selection of Asian-inspired noodle dishes including ramen, udon, and stir-fried noodles. Each bowl is made fresh with flavorful broths, seasonal vegetables, and your choice of protein, making it a warm and satisfying option any time of day.

Wolftown

Wolftown serves Mexican-inspired food such as burritos, tacos, nachos, quesadillas, and rice bowls. Students can customize their meals by choosing different proteins, toppings, and sauces.

One of the most popular menu items is the California burrito because of its large portion size and flavorful ingredients. Wolftown is usually busy during lunch and dinner hours because it is affordable and filling.

Makai

Makai offers Hawaiian-inspired food with fresh ingredients and colorful presentation. Students can order rice bowls with grilled chicken, pork, or seafood along with vegetables and tropical sauces.

The restaurant is known for its balanced meals and refreshing flavors. Many students enjoy eating here because the bowls feel healthier while still being filling and satisfying.

Crave

Crave specializes in burgers, sandwiches, fries, and comfort food. Students often visit Crave for quick meals that are warm, crispy, and satisfying after long classes.

The loaded fries and chicken sandwiches are especially popular among students. Crave is usually one of the busiest locations during dinner time because of its large portions and casual atmosphere.

Rooftop

Rooftop is Sixth College's open-air dining terrace, offering stunning views of the campus alongside a rotating selection of grilled items, bowls, and seasonal specials. Its relaxed outdoor atmosphere makes it a favorite spot for students looking to enjoy a meal with a view.

Overall Sixth College Dining Rating

Canyon Vista Marketplace

Location: Canyon Vista Marketplace - Warren College

Located in Warren College, Canyon Vista Marketplace is UCSD's largest halal-certified residential dining facility. It offers healthy, globally inspired meals centered around UCSD Dining's "Rooted in Flavor" concept, along with groceries, snacks, grab-and-go items, and household essentials. The dining hall overlooks the canyon and serves as a major social and dining hub for students.

View Today's Menu

Stations inside Canyon Vista Marketplace

Fusion Grill

Fusion Grill is Canyon Vista's classic grill station serving made-to-order comfort food with global influences, including burgers, sandwiches, fries, and rotating specials. It is known for hearty halal-certified options and customizable meals.

Three Sixty (360) & 360 Quick Service

Three Sixty is Canyon Vista's international noodle and rice bowl station featuring customizable dishes with flexible sauces, seared proteins, and globally inspired flavors. The station focuses on quick, made-to-order halal-certified meals. 360 Quick Service is the fast-grab counter of Three Sixty, offering ready-made global cuisine and quick takeaway meals for students on the go between classes.

Earl's Coffeehouse

Earl's Coffeehouse offers coffee, espresso drinks, pastries, and light café-style snacks in a relaxed, study-friendly environment. It also connects to the market section where students can purchase grab-and-go foods and essentials.

Fresh

Fresh is Canyon Vista's health-focused station offering salads, wraps, grain bowls, and lighter meal options made with fresh ingredients. The menu emphasizes balanced, sustainable, and customizable meals.

Overall Canyon Vista Marketplace Rating
