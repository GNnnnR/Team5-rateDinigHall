About TritonEats

TritonEats is a website designed to help Tritons explore campus dining halls. UCSD has many dining halls across campus, but it can be hard to know which location has the best meals, restaurants, or menu items.

This website organizes UCSD dining halls by location, shows the restaurants inside each dining hall, and provides descriptions and images of different dishes. Tritons can also rate meals based on taste, value, appearance, and overall experience.

By collecting student ratings, TritonEats helps Tritons make better dining choices and gives a clearer picture of which foods are most popular across campus.
